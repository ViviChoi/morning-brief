Glossary
Allocation
How your money is split across positions, in percent of total current value.
P&L %
Profit or loss compared to your average purchase price. Green = gain, red = loss.
Concentration risk
One holding being too large a share of your portfolio. If it drops, your whole portfolio drops with it.
