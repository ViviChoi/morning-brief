Glossary — what these terms actually mean
VIX
Wall Street's fear gauge. Above 25 = market expects unusually large daily swings.
DXY
U.S. Dollar Index. Higher = dollar strong → headwind for stocks and BTC.
Funding rate
What perpetual-futures longs pay shorts (or vice versa). Positive = traders bullish & paying to hold longs.
Long/Short ratio
Share of retail futures accounts net long vs net short. >1 = more longs than shorts.
ETF net flow
Money entering (positive) or leaving (negative) U.S. spot BTC ETFs today, in USD millions.
F&G
Composite "how scared / how greedy is the crowd" index. 0 = panic, 100 = euphoria.
